About NamasteLang

NamasteLang is a fun, beginner-friendly programming language that adds a Hindi twist to coding! Developed by Kenedy Duwarah, BCA student, it lets users write programs using Hindi-based commands with simple, easy-to-understand syntax. Designed for learning, experimentation, and fun, NamasteLang is perfect for coding newbies or anyone looking to try something unique. Whether you're a Hindi speaker or just curious, NamasteLang makes coding accessible, relatable, and enjoyable!
